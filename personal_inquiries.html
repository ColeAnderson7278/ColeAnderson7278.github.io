Object-Oriented Design

I got into this type of design around two months into the program, when my instructor gave me Practical Object-Oriented Design In Ruby to read. This book doesn't show me new tools to use; however, it gives me a new way to view, think, and, above all, design my code. After reading this book, I began working on a personal side project called Practicing OOD. For this project, I reread this book, which is written for Ruby, and then rewrote the coding example in Python. I also explain my reasons for doing so in my commit statements.
